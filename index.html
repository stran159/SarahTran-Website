Sociology

Currently a first year Sociology major attending the University of California, Riverside, I am working to learn more about the society that we live in and how it effects the way we treat it. Previously, in my high school expderience, I was the co-editor in chief of Huntington Beach High School's "Slick News" and, with this experience, became a media intern with CALPIRG Students, one of many chapters at UCR.

Through our work at CALPIRG Students, we focus on climate change, zero hunger, and textbook affordability for UCR students and the state of California. As a media intern, I run classes on how to write opinion editorials so that we can expand our voice to beyond just UCR students. We do this by striving to work in conjunction with other news publications around the community such as "The Highlander," "The Press Enterprise," and "Inland Empire Magazine."

More specifically, I work directly with the 100% Renewable Energy campaign apart of CALPIRG Students, which focuses on achieving 100% clean energy by the year 2030 through the SB100 bill that is currently set by 2045. As a collective, we are trying to push this deadline closer to 2030 because we ssimply do not have enough time to wait another 14 years. Other than CALPIRG Students, I am also apart of many other clubs and organizations such as The Vietnamese Student Association and The Women's Network chapter at UCR.
